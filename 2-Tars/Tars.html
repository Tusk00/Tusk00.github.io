Tars the self balancing robot

Over the lockdown i decided to buya 3D printer, I orginally wanted to build a 6-axis robot arm but once it was built I couldn't to work properly and that was without any code. So i decided to find something a little simpler and came upon this self balancing robot based on Boston Dynamics two wheeled self balancing robot. I decided to name it after a robot from the best movie of all time Interstellar.

Im using 4 20kg servo motors for the arms which act as limbs so it can move up or down as well as rotate the body, Tars has 2 Nema 17 motors powering the wheels, it will have an ultrasonic sensor in the near future to avoid running into objects, and the most important part a gyroscope board to control the tilt so it can stay upright.

This is all run by a raspberyy pi 4 and an arduino with a cnc shield. I decided to use A4988 stepper motor driver as they are compatable with the cnc shield, and the PCA9685 Servo motor driver as one board can hold up to 12 servos.

This is still a work in progress. Im currently working on connecting all the boards, motors, and sensors together. Once finished I will upload more pictures and a video of it up and running.
