Employee Payroll/Invoices

This program is used to keep track of people who work for you and you can also add invoices. It adds together the prices of the invoices, and how ever much each employee makes on their paycheck.

For employees you can track their hire date, id number, life insurance company name, life insurance amount, dependents, and their pay.

You also have the option to add employees to workgroups. You can make one of the employees a supervisor and it displays all employees in the workgroup with their id numbers.

The invoices display the name of the item bought, how many you bought, the price of each item, and the total for all of the items. At the very bottom I included the total amount of money that is going to be paid for this pay period.
