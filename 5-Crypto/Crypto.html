Mining Rig

I decided a few months ago to build a cryptocurrency mining rig to make some extra money. I searched for graphics cards for about a month and a half until i came upon 7 RX580 which I bought and are currently in use.

I currently have 7 RX580 8gb and 1 1060 6gb, which have all been overclocked to make a total of about 230 Mh. It has been running very smoothly for about two weeks now mining Ethereum.

I used a wire rack to hold the graphics cards and 3D printed a mount that the wire rack goes into on one end and the graphics card screws into the other side with a wood screw insert and holds all 8 very well.

The motherboard, cpu, ram, and ssd are powered with an old 550 watt power supply I had. The graphics cards which take up about 800 watts are powered with a 2600 watt server power supply. This power supply only runs off of 220v so I had to install that as well.

I am running this on linux hiveOS mostly because linux is much more stable than windows and this is gonna be running 24/7 and I dont want it crashing.
