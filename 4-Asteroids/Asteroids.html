Asteroids

I decided to take on a bigger project and came across asteroids. This was my first game made using pygame coded in python aswell and i would say it was a success.

This is just like the old Asteroids game when you shoot a big asteroid it breaks into 2 and then those break into 3 each. The asteroids move at a random velocity and start in a random spot but a certain distance away from the spaceship so you dont die right away.

The spaceship moves like its in space where there is no gravity the ship will just keep going. I should add a brake to the ship, there isn't one currently you have to flip the ship and go the other way to slow down. The ship resembles the gardians of the galaxy ship which i really like.
